I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the City University of Hong Kong and advised by Professors [Kede Ma](https://kedema.org/) (马柯德), [Ying Wei](https://wei-ying.net/) (魏颖), and [Yu Zhang](https://yuzhanghk.github.io/) (张宇). My research centers on building adaptive, efficient, and evolving AI systems, with current interests in agentic memory, efficient LLM adaptation (PEFT, RLVR, KV cache), deep generative models, transfer/meta/continual learning, and AI for science.

Before joining CityUHK, I received my B.Eng. from SUSTech, with early explorations in high-performance computing, augmented reality, blockchain, games, and software engineering.
