I am currently a Ph.D. candidate at the City University of Hong Kong and advised by Professors Kede Ma (马柯德), Ying Wei (魏颖), and Yu Zhang (张宇). My research centers on building adaptive, efficient, and evolving AI systems, with current interests in agentic memory, efficient LLM adaptation (PEFT, RLVR, KV cache), deep generative models, transfer/meta/continual learning, and AI for science.

Before joining CityUHK, I received my B.Eng. from SUSTech, with early explorations in high-performance computing, augmented reality, blockchain, games, and software engineering.
